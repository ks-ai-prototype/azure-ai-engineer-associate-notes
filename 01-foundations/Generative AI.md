## 1. What is Generative AI

### 1.1 Definition

**Generative AI** is a type of Artificial Intelligence that **creates new content**  
(text, code, images, audio, etc.) by learning patterns from large datasets.

### 1.2 Key Points (for notes)

- Learns from **examples** (data)  
- Generates **new** content â€“ not just selecting existing content  
- Works **probabilistically** (based on likelihood, not fixed rules)
